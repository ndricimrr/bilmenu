How do I submit a missing meal photo?
Use the "Submit" tab in the app, take a photo of the missing meal, and fill out the form. Your submission helps improve the meal database for all students.
Why don't I see today's meals?
Meal plans are updated daily. If you don't see today's meals, try refreshing the app or check back later. Sometimes there might be a delay in the cafeteria posting the menu.
How do I enable meal notifications?
Go to Settings in the app and toggle on "Meal Notifications". You can customize when you want to receive reminders about meal times.
Is this app official from Bilkent University?
No, BilMenu is an unofficial app created by students for students. It's not affiliated with Bilkent University, but it helps students stay informed about cafeteria meals.
How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?
You can email us at bilmenudeveloper@gmail.com with your feedback, or visit our Contributions page to learn how to contribute to the project.
